Case Studies

Rate That Dev : Guerilla research obtained by presenting our working prototype to Recruiters and CTOs indicated that decision makers were excited about our Developer Peer Ratings App but looking additionally to:
  • View a VIDEO of developers actually presenting an idea or feature and
  • Be able to perform BOOLEAN SEARCHES when searching for candidates (ex: Search 'React' OR 'Angular').
    • [View the Powerpoint Presentation]



    Members Only App :
  • User Research indicated that Dr. Naumes' clients were excited about the app as a place to keep all health and billing information in one central place, but would tend to use only a few of the proposed features.
  • User Research also provided the idea for two previously unconsidered features - "Message with Doctor" and "Refer a Friend".




    • E-Commerce Shop > Meal Plans :
  • Interviews with users indicated that though urban women of childbearing age (typically working or caretaking) were genuinely interested in cooking healthy meal plans specifically for their health concern/condition, they had difficulty finding the time to cook the large number of highly specific meals included in the plan. This observation along with the rise of deep-pocketed competing products/services (ex:Sun Basket) led to the organization deprioritizing the marketing of the product.
